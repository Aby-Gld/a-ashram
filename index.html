Every Child Deserves a Chance

In many low-income communities in Nagpur, children grow up without access to stable education, safe childcare, or the opportunity to realize their full potential.

Without support, these children often fall behind before their lives even begin.

A Place Where Children Can Grow

Antarbharti Ashram is a family-run school and childcare center dedicated to helping children from underprivileged families.

We provide education, care, guidance, and a safe environment where children can learn, dream, and build a better future.
