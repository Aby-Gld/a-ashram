About Antarbharti Ashram

Learn more about our mission and the impact we create every day.

Who We Are

Antarbharti Ashram is a family-run school in Nagpur, India, dedicated to providing quality education and comprehensive support to children from low-income families.

We believe that every child, regardless of their economic background, deserves access to education, care, and the opportunity to realize their full potential.

Our Vision

We envision a society where every child has access to quality education and the opportunity to grow into confident, compassionate, and capable individuals who contribute positively to their communities.

What We Do

At Antarbharti Ashram, we provide:

  • Free quality education in a nurturing environment
  • Nutritious meals to support healthy development
  • Healthcare support and wellness programs
  • A safe space where children feel valued and supported
  • Dedicated teachers who believe in every child's potential

Our Impact

Through your support, we have transformed the lives of countless children. Every donation enables us to:

  • Expand our educational programs
  • Provide better resources and learning materials
  • Support children's health and nutrition
  • Create a brighter future for our community

Join Our Mission

Your support makes a real difference. By donating to Antarbharti Ashram, you become part of a movement that believes every child deserves opportunity, dignity, and hope.
