Welcome to A-Ashram

Antarbharti Ashram is a family-run sanctuary in Nagpur, India, dedicated to providing quality education, nourishing meals, and compassionate care for children from low-income families. Here, every child is seen, valued, and given the chance to flourish.

"True wisdom is found not in books alone, but in the patience and love of a devoted teacher — the heart of A-Ashram."

Our Vision

We envision a society where every child has access to quality education and the opportunity to grow into a confident, compassionate, and capable individual who contributes positively to their community.

What We Offer

At Antarbharti Ashram, we provide:

  • Free quality education in a nurturing environment
  • Nutritious meals to support healthy development
  • Healthcare support and wellness programs
  • A safe space where children feel valued and supported
  • Dedicated teachers who believe in every child's potential

Our Impact

Through your generosity, we have transformed the lives of countless children. Every donation enables us to:

  • Expand our educational programs
  • Provide better resources and learning materials
  • Support children's health and nutrition
  • Create a brighter future for our community

Join Our Mission

Your support makes a real difference. By contributing to Antarbharti Ashram, you become part of a movement that believes every child deserves opportunity, dignity, and hope.
