About Antarbharti Ashram

Meet the heart and soul behind A-Ashram — people who give everything for the children they serve.

Who We Are

Antarbharti Ashram is a family-run sanctuary in Nagpur, India, dedicated to providing quality education and comprehensive support to children from low-income families.

We believe that every child, regardless of their economic background, deserves access to education, care, and the opportunity to realize their full potential.

The Heart of Our Ashram

At the core of A-Ashram is a compassionate leader who has dedicated her life to nurturing every child who walks through our doors. Her vision, warmth, and unwavering commitment inspire everything we do.

"Every child who enters our ashram carries infinite potential. Our role is simply to light the way." — Archana Hote, Founder

Our Mission

We believe every child deserves a chance to learn, grow, and thrive. At Antarbharti Ashram, we provide safe, nurturing spaces and quality education for children who need it most.

Our Vision

We envision a society where every child has access to quality education and the opportunity to grow into a confident, compassionate, and capable individual who contributes positively to their community.

What We Do

At Antarbharti Ashram, we provide:

  • Free quality education in a nurturing environment
  • Nutritious meals to support healthy development
  • Healthcare support and wellness programs
  • A safe space where children feel valued and supported
  • Dedicated teachers who believe in every child's potential

Our Team

Our dedicated staff and volunteers make our vision a reality every day.
